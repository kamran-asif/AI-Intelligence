# Warehouse Safety & Hazard Procedures

**Reference ID:** SOP-WH-SAF-05  
**Version:** 4.0  
**Effective Date:** May 1, 2026  
**Owner:** Environmental Health & Safety (EHS) Officer  

## 1. General Safety Gear (PPE)
All warehouse personnel and visitors must wear standard Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) at all times within the active operating zones:
- **Steel-toed boots**: Mandatory in all zones (Zones A, B, C, D).
- **High-visibility safety vest**: Class 2 safety vest required.
- **Safety helmets (Hard Hats)**: Mandatory when operating or walking near high-density storage racks (Zone A and Zone D).
- **Protective gloves**: Required when handling metallic components or unloading pallets.

## 2. Forklift & Vehicle Operations
- Forklift operators must hold an active certification card.
- Speed limit inside the warehouse is strictly capped at 5 mph (8 km/h).
- Forklifts must yield the right-of-way to pedestrians at all times.
- Horns must be sounded at all corridor intersections and blind corners.

## 3. Spill Response Protocol (Zone C and Zone Q)
In the event of chemical or hydraulic oil leakage:
1. **Isolate**: Clear the immediate area and set up safety cones.
2. **Identify**: Determine the substance type from the safety data sheet (SDS) folder.
3. **Contain**: Apply absorbents from the spill kit located at Station 4 (Zone C).
4. **Report**: Notify the EHS officer at extension 404 immediately.
5. **Dispose**: Double-bag contaminated absorbents and transfer them to Hazardous Storage.
