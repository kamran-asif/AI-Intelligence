# Warehouse Vendor Contracts & SLAs

**Reference ID:** CON-WH-VEN-04  
**Version:** 1.5  
**Effective Date:** April 10, 2026  
**Owner:** Vendor Procurement Coordinator  

## 1. Prime Supplier Agreements
The warehouse works with three preferred suppliers for industrial supply chain fulfillment.

### Supplier A: Apex Machinery Corp
- **Supplies**: Industrial Gears (Class A), Control Panels (Class A)
- **Lead Time**: 5 business days
- **SLA Threshold**: 98% on-time delivery
- **Penalty Terms**: If delivery is delayed past 7 days, a 2% discount is applied automatically to the invoice.

### Supplier B: HydraFlow Solutions
- **Supplies**: Hydraulic Valves (Class A)
- **Lead Time**: 7 business days
- **SLA Threshold**: 95% on-time delivery
- **Order Constraints**: Minimum order quantity of 10 units.

### Supplier C: ElectraWire Ltd
- **Supplies**: Copper Wiring (Class B), Rubber Seals (Class C)
- **Lead Time**: 3 business days
- **SLA Threshold**: 99% on-time delivery.

## 2. General SLA Discrepancies
- **Damaged Shipments**: Any discrepancies or damaged shipments must be logged and reported to the supplier within 3 business days to qualify for free replacements.
- **Urgent Restocking**: In case of emergency stockouts, Suppliers can provide 24-hour expedited shipping for a flat surcharge of $150 per pallet.
